Surface is when the mask off animation starts and at greater than 0m.
Underwater is when the mask is on and equal to or less than 0m.

Fire equates to danger.

Oxygen is when the oxygen warning is triggered and the ui appears.

Redweed entered when soft stinger/sound design plays.
Redweed exit when stinger/sound design stops.

Safe reef entrance can be hard to judge, but listen to the tone of the sound design, the depth and the lighting.
